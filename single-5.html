Cheese Package

Package

Luna Feast is a luxury cheese brand. The products of this brand are different avored cheese and accessories.

The logo consists of a little mouse and a bright moon. Mouse and moon are two elements related to cheese. The moon looks like cheese and cheese is mouse’s favorite food. A wide variety of different cheeses is a feast for a mouse. There are suggestions on the package to tell which kinds of food are suitable with this cheese. There are different colors and some hand drawn patterns on the package as well to show the avor.

The color of cheese is bright but the color of accessories is soft. The color difference can distinguish the different sets.
