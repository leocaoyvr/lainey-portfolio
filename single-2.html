Absolut Vodka Poster

Poster

These three posters are made for Absolut Vodka, which is renowned for its creative ads.

Each poster, consists of a table, a bottle and a background, uses a different kind of fruit to illustrate the avor and the meaning of the corresponding wine. The customers can see the fruits through the transparent bottles. And in the background, there are some elements which relate to the fruits.

For instance, apple tree, strawberry leaves and water melon peels. The strawberry, watermelon and apple respectively implies vitality, refreshment and happiness, which are how the customers feel when they drink the wines. The traits that these posters share in common are, they are all fruits, and their dominant colors are all red. Moreover, the red fruits and green elements in the background deliberately create a sharp contrast effect on the posters.
